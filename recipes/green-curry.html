Green Curry Recipe

Green curry is a delicious traditional Thai curry. The name green curry comes from the color of the dish and the peppers that bring it's flavor.

Ingredients

  • Thai basil
  • Bamboo shotos
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green curry paste
  • Makrut lime leaves
  • Coconut milk
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Chicken stock, unsalted

Steps:

  1. Pound green curry paste and Thai basil leaves
  2. Get the paste as fine as possible
  3. Reduce the coconut milk until very think
  4. Add the curry paste and saute for a few minutes
  5. Toss chicken with the curry paste
  6. Add coconut milk
  7. Add makrut lime leaves
  8. Add fish sauce and palm sugar
  9. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is fork tender
  10. Add bamboo shoots and cook for a minute until the curry comes to a boil
  11. Stir in red bell pepper and turn off the heat
  12. Add Thai basil and stir just until wilted
  13. The green curry is now ready to serve with jasmine rice
